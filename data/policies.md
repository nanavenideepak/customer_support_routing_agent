# TechNest Store — Policies

## Refund Policy
- Refunds are eligible within 30 days of delivery
- Refund processing takes 5–7 business days after approval
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Items must be unused and in original packaging for full refund
- Damaged or missing items are eligible for immediate escalation

## Return Policy
- Returns accepted within 15 days of delivery
- Customer must raise a return request via email or phone
- Reverse pickup is arranged within 2 business days

## Shipping Policy
- Standard delivery: 3–5 business days
- Express delivery: 1–2 business days (additional charge)
- Free shipping on orders above Rs 2000
- Tracking details shared within 24 hours of dispatch
